# Case Study: StockOps Inventory Platform

**Subtitle:** An inventory and order management platform for products, warehouses, stock movement, invoices, and operational reporting.

## Problem
Growing product-based businesses often manage inventory, purchase orders, sales orders, invoices, warehouses, and stock movement through spreadsheets, phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and disconnected tools. This creates stock mismatches, late reorder decisions, poor warehouse visibility, manual reporting, and avoidable operational errors. Operations teams scale poorly when their data is fragmented across various informal channels.

## Solution
StockOps centralizes inventory operations into one unified SaaS platform with comprehensive product management, live stock tracking, warehouse visibility, complete purchase and sales order workflows, invoice status tracking, stock movement history, automated low-stock alerts, and dynamic operational dashboards. By bridging the gap between physical warehouses and procurement teams, it brings organization and clarity to complex supply chains.

## Key Features
- **Product Catalogue:** Centralized truth for SKUs, pricing, supplier links, and categorization.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Real-time visibility of stock counts across multiple warehouse locations.
- **Low-Stock Alerts:** Automated queues highlighting items that have dropped below user-defined threshold limits.
- **Warehouse Management:** Tracking facility capacities, locations, and managers.
- **Purchase Order Workflow:** Draft, send, approve, and receive workflows for supplier purchasing.
- **Sales Order Workflow:** Process fulfillment from initial order to delivery and invoicing.
- **Supplier and Customer Records:** Centralized directory for B2B relationship management.
- **Invoice Status Tracking:** Unified view for payables and receivables directly linked to their originating orders.
- **Stock Movement Timeline:** Audit trail of all adjustments, transfers, and incoming/outgoing stock.
- **Reports and Analytics:** Dashboard metrics tracking inventory valuation, sales vs. purchase volume, and warehouse performance over time.
- **Role-Based Platform Structure:** Different access contexts (Admin, Warehouse Manager, Sales, Purchasing) built into the UI.

## Business Value
- **Better Stock Visibility:** Eliminate manual spreadsheet updates and prevent over-selling.
- **Reduced Operational Friction:** Replace WhatsApp and email chains with systemic workflows.
- **Faster Reorder Decisions:** Low-stock panels immediately trigger Purchase Order creation.
- **Cleaner Warehouse Tracking:** Understand capacity loads and distribute inventory efficiently.
- **Improved Order Management:** Link fulfillment directly with stock decrements.
- **Better Invoice Visibility:** Ensure no payment or receivable falls through the cracks.
- **Stronger Operational Control:** Single source of truth for the entire product lifecycle.
- **Scalable Foundation:** Built on modern web architecture capable of supporting growing B2B teams.
